# Strategic Standards

## Core Qualification Principle
- Qualification is based on **system state** and **buyer mindset**, not company size.
- Never disqualify by employee count, revenue tier, or "enterprise-only" assumptions.

## Valid Buyer Entry States
- **Broken system:** Existing stack is fragmented, leaky, and unreliable.
- **Missing system:** Operator has no coherent system yet and needs a practical foundation path.

Both states are valid commercial paths.

## Positioning Standard
- Lead with systems thinking: diagnose -> structure -> implement -> operate.
- Describe outcomes as system-level shifts, not isolated marketing tasks.
- Keep language practical and operator-relevant.

## Disallowed Drift
- Size-based anti-persona framing ("too small unless enterprise").
- Generic agency filler ("full-service", "tailored solutions", "we do everything").
- Strategy language without operational path or proof.

## Required Strategic Signals
- Explicitly identify buyer state or state transition.
- Show how the page/service/tool maps to problems, proof, and next CTA.
- Include trust-stage appropriate ask (do not over-ask at low trust).
