# Proof Page Prompt Contract

Use this prompt to create or revise proof pages so they stay proof-first and system-change-first.

Before planning, load and apply:
- `agent-system/context/strategic_standards.md`
- `agent-system/context/positioning_rules.md`
- `agent-system/context/buyer_psychology.md`
- `agent-system/context/strategic_scorecard.md`
- `agent-system/context/problem_service_mapping.md`
- `agent-system/context/trust_ladder_ctas.md`
- `agent-system/context/page_generation_rules.md`
- `agent-system/context/page-purpose-boundaries.md`
- `agent-system/context/relationship_display_limits.md`
- `agent-system/context/proof_storytelling_rules.md`

Rules:
- Proof pages answer: what was broken/missing, what changed, what outcomes improved, what to do next.
- Do not turn proof into client promotion or a “tools used” list.
- Related entities must remain subordinate to the story (caps apply).
- Prefer one “next step” bridge over many related sections.

Required output (scannable, results-first):
1. Stakes (1 screen)
2. Pre-state (symptoms + operational reality)
3. Intervention (system changes shipped)
4. Outcomes (with context; no vanity metrics)
5. Why it worked (brief principles)
6. Next step (one primary CTA + optional secondary)
7. Curated related links (within caps)

Asset note:
- If proof needs new visuals, switch to `asset_system` and use `asset-brief-prompt.md` and/or `component-visual-prompt.md`.
